About Us

The Shared Value Program

At Practera we run a virtual 2-week student employability program at top Australian universities to engage teams of students to do market research and provide recommendations on how to scale your organisation’s impact in sustainable ways.

Shared value is policies and operating practices that enhance competitiveness and simultaneously enhance economic and societal conditions of a business/organisation.


How It Works

  • Step 1Submit a project.
  • Step 2Practera assigns a student team to your project.
  • Step 3Students work on your project whilst Practera oversees their work.
  • Step 4You receive tailored market research report.
  • Attend a virtual 30-min briefing session
  • Spend 1-2hrs/week to review students’ work and provide online feedback
